River Winds has moved to its new home in the Bothell/Woodinville region!

Recently moving from Duvall to Bothell, the River Winds Band is an all-volunteer, non-profit organization of over 45 members from the Northshore region of King County, servicing Bothell, Woodinville, Kenmore and surrounding communities. Under the direction of John Werth, the River Wind Band members are comprised of serious amateur musicians within the community and include music teachers, musical instrument technicians, business and medical professionals, moms, dads and even grandparents!

The band rehearses every Tuesday evening from 7:30-9:15pm, and performs three formal concerts yearly in the beautiful Northwest Community Church pavilion. The new location is conveniently located just off the 405/522 interchange; the Church being located inside the Northcreek Business Park at 11715 N Creek Pkwy S # 110, Bothell.

The River Winds Band encourages our new community to follow and support our band's growth and performances this new season. Our calendar will be announced shortly, although our Winter Concert will be in November. We look forward to seeing you at our coming concerts!

We are now part of your community, and we appreciate your support!
