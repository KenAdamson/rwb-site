Welcome to River Winds Concert Band

River Winds has concluded our concert season.
Thank you for your support, and we look forward to seeing you for our 2015-2016 Concert Season!

The River Winds is a collection of musicians who live mainly in the Snoqualmie Valley, but have members from as far as Seattle and Bremerton. We are music educators, musical instrument technicians, business and medical professionals, moms, dads- even granparents! We are part of your community, and we appreciate your support.

We are a non-profit adult community band based in the Duvall/Carnation area. These people, many of whom hadn't played in years before we started, meet every Tuesday from 7-9 pm in the Cedarcrest music room and do an amazing thing: in today's hectic world, they find time to get together and make music.

Join Us!

Even though some sections are full at the moment, we often have members who must take time off for various reasons and need new musicians to fill those positions. Part of our mission is to bring music into the lives of our community members, so don't be shy - we want you!
