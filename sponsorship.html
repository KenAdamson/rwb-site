Want to help our organization?

The River Winds Concert Band is a IRS certified, 501-(c)-3 non-profit organization. Funds are raised from a variety of sources, including the general public, band members (via dues and contributions), corporations and foundations, public funds, and other organizations interested in promoting community-based concert music. Monetary contributions in any amount are welcome, and donors will be recognized unless they wish to remain anonymous.

If you're interested in making a cash donation to support the band's efforts (note that our band members are not paid), you can choose from the following options:

Via a check written to the "River Winds Band" and mailed to:

River Winds Band
PO Box 1620
Duvall, WA 98019-1620

Via online secure credit card donation through PayPal
(Please click the PayPal button in the upper right of your screen)

We will send you a receipt for your donation as soon as we receive it.
